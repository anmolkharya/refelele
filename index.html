Proposed PhD Framework

Based on the analysis, here is a structured pathway for your thesis.

Primary Objectives

  • Develop a hybrid model integrating informal waste pickers into the formal E-waste value chain using digital tracking.
  • Quantify the economic impact of "segregation at source" policies in Tier-2 Indian cities (referencing Indore model).
  • Assess the viability of AI-driven sorting mechanisms in low-resource municipal contexts.

Novel Contribution

Most existing literature (Menon, 2022; Gupta, 2024) focuses on either MSW logistics or E-waste policy. Your PhD can bridge this by proposing a Unified Circularity Index that measures efficiency across both streams in urban environments.
